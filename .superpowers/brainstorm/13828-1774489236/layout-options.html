Sphere Layout Strategy

How should agent spheres be arranged in the terminal space?

A. Orbital

Root at center, children orbit around it. Grandchildren orbit their parent. Shows hierarchy through spatial proximity. Most visually striking but requires collision avoidance.

B. Gravity Pack

Spheres cluster toward center, pushed apart by radius. No hierarchy encoding — just a lava-lamp-like cluster of activity. Simplest to implement, most organic feel.

C. Tree Flow

Left-to-right flow mirroring the agent tree. Root on left, children branching right. Preserves hierarchy explicitly but more rigid — feels more like a diagram than art.
